THUS SANG THE TREES

Thus Sang the Trees is a result of multiple masters theseis. My research in relations of humans and other agents was used as a basis for its vision. The project is centered around the players interaction with the plant world w hich is needed for them in order to reach the end. The other elements are eldritch/gothic horror to underline breaking the anthropocentric conventions in video games.
